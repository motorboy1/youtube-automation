# Channel Analytics Dashboard

## Overview
Comprehensive analytics platform for YouTube creators. Get deep insights into your channel performance, audience behavior, and revenue metrics.

## Key Metrics
- **Views & Watch Time**: Total and trending metrics
- **Subscriber Growth**: Daily, weekly, monthly tracking
- **Engagement Rate**: Likes, comments, shares analysis
- **Revenue Tracking**: AdSense and sponsorship income
- **Audience Demographics**: Age, location, device data
- **Traffic Sources**: Where your viewers come from

## AI-Powered Insights
- Optimal upload time recommendations
- Content trend predictions
- Audience retention analysis
- Competitor benchmarking
- Growth opportunity identification

## Competitor Analysis
Track and compare:
- Channel growth rates
- Content strategies
- Engagement metrics
- Upload frequency
- Video performance

## Custom Reports
Create custom reports with:
- Date range selection
- Metric filtering
- Export to PDF/Excel
- Scheduled email delivery
- Team sharing capabilities

## Integration
- YouTube Analytics API
- Google AdSense
- Social media platforms
- Third-party analytics tools
