# AI Script Generator

## Overview
Generate compelling YouTube video scripts using advanced AI technology. Perfect for creators who need consistent, high-quality content across multiple niches.

## Key Features
- **AI-Powered Generation**: State-of-the-art language models
- **Niche Templates**: Pre-optimized for different content types
- **SEO Integration**: Built-in keyword optimization
- **Tone Customization**: Match your brand voice
- **Multi-Language**: Support for 25+ languages
- **Export Options**: TXT, PDF, and DOCX formats

## Supported Niches
- Technology & Reviews
- Lifestyle & Vlogging
- Education & Tutorials
- Entertainment & Comedy
- Gaming & Esports
- Finance & Business

## Script Structure
Each generated script includes:
1. **Hook** (0-15 seconds): Attention-grabbing opening
2. **Introduction** (15-45 seconds): Set context and value proposition
3. **Main Content**: Structured body with key points
4. **Call-to-Action**: Engagement and conversion elements

## Tips for Best Results
- Provide specific, detailed topics
- Include target keywords naturally
- Specify desired video length accurately
- Choose appropriate tone for your audience
- Review and personalize generated content
