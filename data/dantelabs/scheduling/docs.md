# Smart Scheduler

## Overview
AI-powered video publishing scheduler that analyzes your audience behavior to recommend optimal upload times and manage your content calendar.

## Features
- **AI Time Optimization**: Machine learning-based recommendations
- **Content Calendar**: Visual planning interface
- **Timezone Support**: Global audience management
- **Recurring Schedules**: Set it and forget it
- **Batch Scheduling**: Schedule multiple videos at once
- **Performance Tracking**: Measure scheduling impact

## Optimization Modes
1. **Max Views**: Schedule for maximum initial views
2. **Max Engagement**: Optimize for likes and comments
3. **Consistent**: Regular upload pattern for algorithm

## Calendar Management
- Drag-and-drop interface
- Color-coded categories
- Conflict detection
- Team collaboration
- Export to Google Calendar

## Best Practices
- Upload 2-3 times per week minimum
- Maintain consistent schedule
- Consider time zones of target audience
- Avoid scheduling during major events
- Front-load important videos early in week

## Analytics Integration
The scheduler learns from:
- Historical view patterns
- Subscriber activity times
- Engagement rates by time
- Competitor upload patterns
- Seasonal trends

## Timezone Strategy
- Identify primary audience location
- Consider secondary markets
- Account for daylight saving time
- Test different time slots
- Monitor performance by region
